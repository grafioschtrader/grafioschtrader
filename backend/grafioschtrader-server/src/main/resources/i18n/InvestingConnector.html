[historical] First find the appropriate instrument (currency, ETF, CFD, etc.) at Investing.
Then click "Historical Data", e.g.S&P 500 (SPX).
To determine the two values curr_id, smlID the network of developer tools must be activated in the web browser.
Entering a date range will trigger an XHR request ttps://www.investing.com/instruments/HistoricalDataAjax.
Under "Form Data" curr_id and smlID can be read, for example curr_id: 166 and smlID: 2030167 for the S&P 500.
After flying the URL of the instrument, curr_id and smlID can be entered separated by commas, e.g. "indices/us-spx-500,166,2030167 for the S&P 500". [intra] Find the appropriate instrument (currency, ETF, CFD, etc.) at Investing.
Then take the part without "https://www.investing.com/" from the URL. For example, from "https://www.investing.com/equities/dropbox-inc" for the stock Dropbox Inc (DBX), only "equities/dropbox-inc" is taken.
